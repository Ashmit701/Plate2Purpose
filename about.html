About Us

Introducing Plate2Purpose: a groundbreaking food redistribution initiative dedicated to reducing food waste and combating hunger. Plate2Purpose provides a comprehensive food waste management system designed to repurpose surplus food in a meaningful way. Through our platform, individuals and businesses can easily donate excess food, which we then collect and redistribute to those in need. With partnerships with over 150 banquets, Plate2Purpose is making a significant impact in addressing both food waste and hunger.
